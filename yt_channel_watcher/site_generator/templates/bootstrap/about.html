About

This is a fan made website. The site only tracks my own taste of channels.
You can't browse older videos or members-only content on this site. Please use YouTube for that.

yt-channel-watcher is a program that call yt-dlp to fetch channels informations (videos&shorts) and then generate a static HTML from the video data.
I really liked the holodex project, but it only/mostly tracks vtuber clippers. I just wanted to see the vtubers' own content.
This project is not limited to just to the vtuber community, you can use it to fetch any youtube channel.
